About

Our team collaborated to create an app that aids in interior design, saving money and time for our users. Posterize is an all-inclusive app that makes seeing your posters, art, mirrors, etc on your walls without ever having to nail a hole in the wall. Posterize is reinventing the world of interior design for professionals or just the average person.

  • We made our app as simple as possible making it quick and easy for the users.
  • Open up our app and hit sign in, fill in your name, email, and password.
  • Once you see the landing page, click the top right of the screen (the camera)
  • Select the image you want to see on your wall.
  • Click see in AR and get creative!
